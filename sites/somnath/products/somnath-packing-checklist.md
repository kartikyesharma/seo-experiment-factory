# Documents and confirmations

- Government-issued photo identification
- Offline accommodation confirmation
- Offline onward and return transport details
- Travel insurance details, if applicable
- Emergency and accommodation phone numbers

# Practical essentials

- Modest, comfortable clothing suited to the forecast
- Comfortable footwear and a simple footwear bag if useful
- Essential medicines in original packaging
- Water bottle where current rules permit it
- Sun and rain protection for the season
- Small payment backup: limited cash plus digital payment

# Before leaving

- Confirm current temple timings and official notices
- Confirm restrictions on bags, phones, cameras, and other belongings
- Check live train, bus, road, or flight status
- Check severe weather and local safety advice
- Share your itinerary with a trusted contact
- Leave a buffer between arrival, the main visit, and departure
