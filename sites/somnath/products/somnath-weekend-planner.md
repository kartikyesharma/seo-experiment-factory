# Before you use this planner

Temple timings, entry rules, ceremonies, local transport, prices, and weather can change. Check the official Shree Somnath Trust website and your transport operator before leaving. This sample is a planning aid, not a booking or official access document.

# A calm two-day outline

## Day one: arrive with a buffer

- Aim to reach your accommodation before the evening rush.
- Keep identity documents and only essential belongings with you.
- Check current entry and security rules before approaching the temple.
- Leave a generous buffer around any ceremony you hope to attend.
- Use the evening for the seafront area only if local weather and safety advice permit.

## Day two: an unhurried circuit

- Start after confirming current opening information.
- Choose one or two nearby places rather than packing in every attraction.
- Keep the final two hours flexible for meals, luggage collection, and station transfer.
- Recheck your train, bus, or flight status directly with the operator.

# Packing checklist

- Government-issued photo identification
- Modest, weather-suitable clothing
- Refillable water bottle where permitted
- Essential medicines and basic sun protection
- A small amount of cash plus a digital payment option
- Offline copies of bookings and emergency contacts

# Questions to confirm before travel

1. What are the current opening and ceremony times?
2. Are phones, bags, footwear, or cameras restricted?
3. Are there closures, special-event crowds, or weather alerts?
4. How will you reach and leave your accommodation after dark?
5. What is your backup transport option?
