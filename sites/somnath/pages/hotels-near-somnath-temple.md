A hotel can look close on a listing while the practical route involves traffic, restricted vehicle access, or a longer walk than expected. Open the map, inspect the surrounding roads, and ask the property how guests usually travel to the temple area.

## Compare the complete stay

Check the full property name and address, recent reviews across more than one source, room type, occupancy, taxes, check-in time, late-arrival handling, parking, luggage, stairs or lifts, and cancellation terms. Save the written confirmation offline.

## Ask about your arrival

A late train or road journey changes what “convenient” means. Confirm whether reception will be open and how you will reach the property safely. If an early visit matters, ask about breakfast, checkout, luggage storage, and local transport without assuming availability.

## Treat help forms as enquiries

Submitting a request on an independent travel site does not reserve a room. Verify the final provider, price, refund conditions, and payment recipient before accepting an offer.
