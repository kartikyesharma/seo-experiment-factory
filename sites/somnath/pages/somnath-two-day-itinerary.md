An itinerary works best as a sequence of priorities, not a promise that every hour will unfold on schedule. Put the main visit first, then add nearby stops only when time and energy allow.

## Day 1: arrive and orient yourself

Settle in, check current official notices, and learn the route between your accommodation and the temple area. If your transport is on time and the current schedule permits, use the evening for an initial visit. Otherwise, keep it for a meal and rest.

## Day 2: main visit and one local choice

Choose a verified morning or later slot, allowing for security and queues. Afterward, select one nearby place based on opening information, heat, and travel time. Protect the final part of the day for luggage and departure.

## What not to over-plan

Avoid stacking distant attractions, non-refundable transfers, and a fixed ceremony time on the same day. A shorter plan with one generous buffer is more useful than a crowded checklist.
