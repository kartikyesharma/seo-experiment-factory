Online searches may combine an official facility, third-party religious services, travel packages, advertisements, and old instructions. Before using any option, establish who provides it and whether the current Shree Somnath Trust portal lists that exact service.

## Begin from the trust portal

Avoid arriving at a payment page through an unknown message or agent link. Navigate from the official portal, read the current instructions, and confirm the date, purpose, devotee details, amount, payment recipient, and acknowledgement process.

## Do not confuse a service with guaranteed access

A pooja-related transaction should not be treated as a guarantee of darshan, a queue position, physical entry, or any benefit that the official terms do not state. Keep claims and receipts in their proper context.

## Protect your information

Share identity and contact information only when the verified process genuinely requires it. Save the confirmation and official support details, and avoid sending sensitive documents or money to unverified personal accounts.
