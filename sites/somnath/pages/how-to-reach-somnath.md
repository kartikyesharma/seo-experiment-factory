The best route depends less on straight-line distance and more on your arrival time, final connection, and tolerance for delays. Compare the **whole door-to-door journey**, not just the longest leg.

## By train

Veraval is a common rail gateway for Somnath. Check the train number, operating date, arrival station, and live status through Indian Railways. Plan the transfer from the station to your stay before arrival, particularly late at night.

## By road

State buses, private buses, taxis, and self-drive routes may connect Somnath with other Gujarat cities. Journey times shown by maps can miss meal breaks, traffic, road works, and seasonal conditions. Avoid building a temple visit immediately after an optimistic arrival time.

## By air

An airport journey still needs a substantial road connection. Compare total time and cost against a train or road journey, then confirm the current flight and ground-transfer options directly with providers.

## The buffer that matters

Keep at least one flexible block between arrival and your main visit. It absorbs late transport, check-in, food, and a current-rules check without turning the day into a race.
