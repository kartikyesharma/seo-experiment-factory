Published schedules are only one part of planning a visit. The useful question is whether a timing is **current for your exact date**, especially around festivals, maintenance, security changes, or large crowds.

## A reliable timing check

1. Open the Shree Somnath Trust website linked below.
2. Look for current notices as well as the regular daily schedule.
3. Recheck shortly before departure and again on the day of your visit.
4. Treat third-party snippets and old screenshots as hints, not confirmation.

## Add time around the stated schedule

Build in time for local traffic, walking from your drop-off point, security checks, queues, and storing items that may not be allowed inside. A tightly connected onward train or bus creates avoidable stress.

## When crowds may be heavier

Weekends, public holidays, major religious dates, and the most popular morning and evening periods may bring longer waits. Current local advisories should take priority over a generic itinerary.
