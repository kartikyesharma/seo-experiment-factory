The temple name appears across official pages, news, maps, travel businesses, social accounts, and independent guides. This page exists to make one boundary unambiguous: **Somnath Trip Notes is not Shree Somnath Trust and does not represent it.**

## Use the primary source for current decisions

Trust notices and the official portal should be your reference for changing schedules, access arrangements, listed online services, and authorised contact information. Our articles explain planning questions but cannot create or modify an official rule.

## Check contact and payment information

Do not copy a phone number, bank account, QR code, or payment link merely because it appears beside the temple name. Compare it with the current official portal and understand exactly what the payment covers before proceeding.

## Separate information from services

Hotels, cabs, travel packages, PDFs, and independent planning assistance are third-party products or services. They must be described and paid for on their own terms without implying trust endorsement.
